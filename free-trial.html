Your free trial includes full access to all features of AI Interview Pro, including unlimited mock interviews, detailed feedback reports, face and voice analysis, and access to all job role simulations. You'll experience everything our premium members get.

Do I need a credit card to sign up?

No, we don't require a credit card to start your free trial. You can sign up and enjoy the full experience without entering any payment information.

What happens after my trial ends?

After your 7-day trial, you'll need to choose a subscription plan to continue using AI Interview Pro. We'll send you reminders before your trial ends so you can decide whether to continue or cancel.

Our standard trial is 7 days, but we occasionally offer extended trials during special promotions. Contact our support team if you need more time to evaluate our platform.

You can cancel anytime during your trial by going to your Account Settings and clicking "Cancel Trial." You'll continue to have access until the trial period ends.
